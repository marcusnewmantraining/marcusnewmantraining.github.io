WHO AM I?

My name is Marcus Newman and I'm a teacher at the Northern College of The Arts & Technology in Preston, Australia.

I am photographer, musician and trainer. I attended RMIT University and completed a Bachelor's Degree in Media Arts.

My Mission

Is to give students the best digital tools the can use to tell their own stories.

Their passion to learn these skills drives me to always be learning, to pass as much as I can to them.
