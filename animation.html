2D Animation

2D Animations may include:

BASIC GAMES

Used to keep users entertained on a website or as part of an advertisement.

BUTTONS

Buttons may be used in several multimedia projects and can contain simple or advanced animation.

CHARACTERS

Animated characters can be used to connect an audience to a product or idea. A character is a person in a narrative work of art (such as a novel, play, or film).

ILLUSTRATIONS

An illustration is a depiction (such as a drawing, painting, photograph, or other image) that is created to elucidate or dictate sensual information (such as a story, poem, or newspaper article).

LOGOS

A logo is a graphic mark or emblem commonly used by commercial enterprises, organisations and even individuals to aid and promote instant public recognition.
