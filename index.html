WHO WE ARE

Killer Sprocket is a family-owned, proudly independent brewery based in Bayswater, Victoria, just east of Melbourne. We have a passion for adventurous, malt-driven ales that transcend style boundaries, grounded in the notion that great beer can be full-flavoured without sacrificing drinkability.
