Marcus Newman

About

G'day and welcome to the website of Marcus Newman, a multidisciplinary artist specialising in education, music, and photography.

Marcus specialises in VCE VET Creative and Digital Media training, guiding students who have received Premiers Awards and have been selected for top designs in the VCE VET. He holds a Post Graduate Diploma of Education and a Certificate IV in Training and Assessment.

In photography, Marcus combines analogue and digital techniques, explores chemigrams, and experiments with unconventional methods like burying photographic film. He has been recognised with awards from the Centre of Contemporary Photography in 2013 and 2014, and holds a bachelor’s degree in Media Arts from RMIT University.

In music, Marcus is a dynamic live electronic artist influenced by techno music. He is known for energetic, improvised performances blending acid house and techno using analogue and digital synthesisers.
